   Pastor, Phil Gerard

Phil was converted when he was five years old and committed his life to full-time service at the age of 17 at Word of Life Camp.

Phil is a graduate of Bob Jones University (B.A., M.A.) and has held pastorates in Mississippi and Pennsylvania before moving to South Carolina in 1997.

Having served as a pastor for over 25 years he has a passion for teaching the Word, seeking to make it simple and relevant. Music is another aspect of ministry close to his heart.

Phil and his wife Jeanne, have three adult children, Justin, Lauren and Meredith, as well as two grandchildren.

Pastor Gerard can be contacted at (864)313-6927

Assistant Pastor, Jon Feusse

Pastor Jon Feusse, a native of Pelzer, served for four years on staff at Camp CoBeAc in Prudenville MI and part time locally as a youth pastor. He married Christina, and in 2007 then served as Associate Pastor of Anchor Baptist Church in South Haven, MI.

God soon blessed them with their first child and in 2010 they returned to the Greenville area so Jon could complete his B.A. in ministerial training at Bob Jones University.

It was during this time that Jon and his family came to MBC desiring to serve God. Upon graduating from BJU, Jon accepted the call as Assistant Pastor at Midway Bible Church.

Pastor Jon and Christina have a heart for local church ministry and desire to see the gospel impact the surrounding community. They have two small children who also love serving the local church.

Pastor Jon can be contacted at (864)357-9345
