Our Mission and Beliefs

Our passion at Midway Bible Church is to see God do something that only He can do. We believe that Jesus Christ is building His church in the Midway community. We desire to be His co-workers in this great work. Jesus Christ builds His church by the means that He has revealed in His Word. Therefore we seek to adhere to a simple, Scriptural philosophy of ministry.

Our Mission is to

  • Proclaim the good news of Jesus Christ in both deed and word.
  • Preach and teach sound doctrine and to equip the saints for the work of the ministry.
  • Assimilate believers into a local body where they can mutually encourage and minister to one another.
  • Passionately worship God in His glory and in our church, our homes, and our persons.

If you are seeking to know God, MBC is here to help you. MBC exists to minister the love of Jesus Christ, to function as the voice, hands and feet of our Savior in the Midway community.

Worship and Fellowship

Come Worship With US

We have a unique worship schedule at Midway Bible Church. On a typical Sunday, we have our morning worship service and our children’s church hour at 10 AM, followed by lunch. We do this so that we have an opportunity to fellowship with each other. After lunch we have a time of “Fellowship around God’s Word” in our Live Application Class. During this time we discuss, in a small group like setting, important lessons and thought provoking questions that arose during the morning message. We also have our children’s and teen’s Sunday School classes during this hour.


Fellowship Meal - Be our guest!

Each Sunday following the 10 AM service, a fellowship meal is served! Come enjoy the finest in southern cuisine and get to know new friends and neighbors around the table.


Prayer Meeting

Each Wednesday, the church comes together for a special time of prayer and BIble study.


Special Events

Throughout the year, MBC hosts annual events from Family Film Nights, to Greenville Drive Games, to a Fall Cookout and Bonfire, and many others!

Children's Ministry

In order to give our children the best Bible teaching possible, Midway holds a children’s church service in conjunction with the main service. This special time gives kids an opportunity for interaction and learning in a context and manner that may be better suited to their age and learning styles.

Good News Club

MBC leads an after-school Bible club at Spearman Elementary School. This club provides an opportunity for children to hear the Gospel and learn truth from God’s Word.

Missions

MBC is thrilled to be a part of the spread of God’s kingdom around the world. We are active in sending missionaries and supporting these missionaries with our money and with our prayers. The five missionary families from Midway Bible Church are located in countries around the world— Cambodia, Papua New Guinea, Ecuador, Mexico, and Haiti. Regularly, a small team from our church travels to Haiti to assist our missionaries in practical projects and evangelistic outreach. We encourage all of our people to respond to God’s call for laborers, whether it means that they serve in a foreign country or in our own community.

Music

God-honoring worship is something we strive for. Many church members get involved in our music ministry. Some play their instruments. Some are vocalists. Others are involve din the choir. These volunteers are an important part of the ministry of Midway Bible Church, using their talents and gifts to glorify God.
