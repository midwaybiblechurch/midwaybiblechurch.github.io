Our Mission

   

Midway Bible Church exists for four purposes:

EVANGELISM

 To proclaim the good news of Jesus Christ in both deed and word

EDIFICATION

 To preach and teach sound doctrine and to equip the saints for the work of the ministry

FELLOWSHIP

 To assimilate believers into a local body where they can mutually encourage and minister to one another

WORSHIP

 To passionately worship God in His glory, in our church, our homes, and our persons
